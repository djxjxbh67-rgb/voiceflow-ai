Why does your business need
a voice AI agent?

Manual customer outreach is expensive, slow, and inefficient. We offer a fundamentally different approach.

😤

The Business Pain

  • One operator handles only 40–60 calls per day
  • 80% of time is wasted on dial-outs and routine answers
  • Call quality degrades toward the end of a shift
  • Cost per contact starts at $2–5 per call
  • Impossible to scale without growing headcount
🚀

Our Solution

  • AI agent handles 1,000+ calls simultaneously
  • Consistent high quality 24/7, no fatigue
  • Cost per contact drops to $0.05–0.15
  • Instant scaling without hiring
  • Full analytics on every single conversation
⚙️ How It Works

Three steps to
automated outreach

From uploading your contact list to a live AI conversation — in under 5 minutes

1

Upload Your Contacts

Import an Excel file with phone numbers and names. Set a System Prompt to define your agent's role, knowledge, and personality.

2

AI Generates the Dialog

Our multi-LLM engine (Gemini, GPT, DeepSeek, YandexGPT) builds responses via streaming. ElevenLabs or Yandex SpeechKit synthesizes the voice.

3

Automated Calling

Voximplant dials the numbers. The AI listens, understands, and responds — just like a real manager. Results are saved in real time.
